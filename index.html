Hello! My name is Jessica Woudsma.


software developer || plant advocate || coffee consumer

Past

Past experiences build upon each other. I've been a farmer, barista, program specialist, slinger of supplements. Photography, food foraging, and hiking are all things I will enjoy my whole life. Simple habits like meditation and reading have also advanced my outlook on how to balance work and rest.

Present

Changing into a career that has been tapping me on the shoulder for the last few years is exciting! Being a software developer, I know I can continually work to solve problems, learn more (and never learn it all), and work with others as well as on my own. It allows creativity, connectivity, and a path toward a broader future.

Future

Helping others in task-oriented ways is a constant with me. Using software development to help people feel more comfortable and confident with what is becoming universal technologies is very important to me. My desire is that my immediate future build from this, and be open to opportunities that might not even exist yet.
